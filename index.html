Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for a subscription plan?

You can sign up on our website or call our customer service to choose the best plan for your needs.

What happens if I need a repair outside of my coverage?

We offer one-time repairs for non-members at a higher deductible. Signing up for a plan provides discounts on out-of-warranty repairs.

How do I schedule a service?

Contact us via our website or call our dispatch line to schedule a technician visit at your convenience.

What happens if my appliance needs to be replaced?

If repair costs exceed the value of a new unit, we offer replacement options or a cash-in-lieu amount.
